Welcome to MEDI-CONNECT

MEDI-CONNECT is a simple online health assistant made by Group 7, First Year B.Tech CSE as a Web Technology mini project. Select your symptoms, enter your basic details, and the site will show you the most likely disease along with commonly prescribed medicines and dosage. It covers 6 illnesses including Viral Fever, Common Cold, Food Poisoning, Migraine, Skin Allergy, and Flu, built using only HTML and CSS.

** IMPORTANT: This is NOT real medical advice. It is only for learning purposes. Always consult a qualified doctor for any health problem. **

Result – Viral Fever / Influenza

Symptoms: Fever, Body Ache, Fatigue, Headache    Severity: Moderate

MedicineDosageWhen to Take
Paracetamol 500mg1 tablet2 times a day after meals
Cetirizine 10mg1 tabletAt night before sleeping

Advice: Bed rest. Drink 3L water daily. Avoid cold food. Visit hospital if fever crosses 103°F.

Result – Common Cold / URI

Symptoms: Cough, Cold, Sore Throat    Severity: Mild

MedicineDosageWhen to Take
Cetirizine 10mg1 tabletAt night
Benadryl Cough Syrup2 teaspoon3 times a day
Strepsils Lozenges1 lozenge3-4 times a day

Advice: Steam inhalation 2x daily. Warm water with ginger and honey. Avoid cold drinks.

Result – Food Poisoning / Gastroenteritis

Symptoms: Stomach Pain, Vomiting, Diarrhea    Severity: Moderate

MedicineDosageWhen to Take
ORS Sachet1 sachetAfter every loose motion
Domperidone 10mg1 tabletBefore every meal
Metronidazole 400mg1 tablet2 times a day for 5 days

Advice: Eat light food like khichdi and banana. Drink boiled water only. Avoid oily/spicy food.

Result – Migraine / Low BP

Symptoms: Headache, Dizziness    Severity: Mild

MedicineDosageWhen to Take
Saridon / Combiflam1 tabletWhen headache starts, after food
Electral Powder1 sachet in waterOnce or twice daily

Advice: Rest in a dark quiet room. Drink plenty of fluids. Avoid screen time during headache.

Result – Allergy / Skin Rash

Symptoms: Skin Rash, Itching    Severity: Mild-Moderate

MedicineDosageWhen to Take
Cetirizine 10mg1 tabletOnce daily at night
Calamine LotionApply gently2-3 times on affected area
Betamethasone CreamThin layerTwice daily on rash

Advice: Avoid scratching. Wear loose cotton clothes. Identify and avoid allergy trigger.

Result – Flu / COVID-like Illness

Symptoms: Fever, Cough, Fatigue, Body Ache    Severity: High

MedicineDosageWhen to Take
Paracetamol 650mg1 tablet3 times a day after meals
Azithromycin 500mg1 tabletOnce daily for 5 days
Vitamin C + Zinc1 tabletOnce daily after breakfast
ORS Sachet1 sachet in 1L waterEvery 3-4 hours

Advice: Isolate yourself. Wear mask. Get COVID test if fever persists. Seek hospital if breathing is difficult.
